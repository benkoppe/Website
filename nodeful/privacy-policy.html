Nodeful Privacy Policy

Nodeful is committed to protecting your attendance data. Nodeful and its developer take your privacy very seriously.

Nodeful uses Google Apps Script to function, therefore Google is able to see all requests made and use that information as it sees fit. Noeful does not use any trackers of any sort, nor does it keep, track, sell, rent, or share any information on you throughout your use of the app. All information shared beyond the scope of the app is contained entirely within the Google Spreadsheet created at setup. The app is open source (available on GitHub), so you’re able to independently verify this.

For any questions, feel free to contact me directly.
