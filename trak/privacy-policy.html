Trak Family Privacy Policy

The Trak family of apps are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use a Trak app. By using Trak, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

Data Collection & Retention

Trak does not collect or store any personally identifiable information on our servers. The only exception to this is with "Pass" features that require dining login, as some information is securely stored to prevent abuse. We respect your privacy and have designed Trak to minimize the data we process. Trak does not use any trackers of any sort, nor does it keep, track, sell, rent, or share any information on you throughout your use of the app. All collected analytical data is rudimentary and completely anonymized.

Location Data

Trak uses your device's location data solely to provide transit routing information. This data is temporarily transmitted to an OpenTripPlanner server that we host to calculate and deliver the routing information you request. This data is not stored, shared, or used for any purpose other than providing routing services.

Third-Party Services

Trak does not share your location data with any third parties. The OpenTripPlanner server is hosted and managed by us to ensure your data remains private.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data during transmission, including using secure connections where applicable. However, no method of data transmission over the internet is completely secure. We strive to protect your data but cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Your Consent

By using Trak, you consent to the processing of your location data as described in this Privacy Policy.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in Trak or legal requirements. Any changes will be effective upon posting the updated policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

For any questions, feel free to contact us directly.

Thank you for using Trak! Your privacy and trust are our top priorities.
